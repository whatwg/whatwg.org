which I might have been responsible, not sure), but hereby a note that

not move them all to the html5 project tree. For licensing reasons,  
because the html5 project owner doesn't like it, and because it just isn't  
worth the trouble.

(If I was unclear about this in the past or have given the impression of  
supporting the opposite view, my apologies.)
