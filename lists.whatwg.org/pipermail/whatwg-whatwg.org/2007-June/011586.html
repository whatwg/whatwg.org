I realize this particular one was answered, but here's a how-to next 
time somebody is looking for something similar.

1) substitute "http://www.ietf.org/internet-drafts/" by 
"http://tools.ietf.org/html/", so visit 
<http://tools.ietf.org/html/draft-hoehrmann-script-types-03.txt>

2) check the "Versions" on top for later drafts or the published RFC

3) tell the IETF to make this variant the default :-)
