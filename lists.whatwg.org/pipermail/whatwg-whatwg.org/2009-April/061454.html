Also, the following words appear with different spelling variations; I suggest one of the variants be picked and used consistently:

behaviour vs. behavior
favorite vs. favourite
honour vs. honor
occurance[s] vs. occurrence[s]
categoris* vs. categoriz*
recognis* vs. recogniz*
serialis* vs. serializ*
tokenis* vs. tokeniz*
