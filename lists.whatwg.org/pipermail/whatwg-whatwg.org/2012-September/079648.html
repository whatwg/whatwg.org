> A proposal has been submitted to create a w3c community group to

After the confusion that occured with the responsive images thread, I just 
want to remind people that if you want changes to the WHATWG 
specifications and want to make sure your feedback gets a response, the 
only forum that we commit to reply to feedback in is this one. Feedback 
sent to other forums, e.g. community groups, may not get looked at and 
will almost certainly not get a direct reply. (Feedback sent in the form 
of bugs will definitely get looked at but may still not get a detailed 
reply.) That said, I don't want to discourage anyone from creating any 
groups they want to create, so long as the above is well understood. :-)
