>

> 
> (function() {
>     var oldCloneNode = HTMLCanvasElement.prototype.cloneNode;

>         var copy = oldCloneNode.call(this, deep);

>         return copy;
>     };

Looks right to me, though generally I would discourage this style of

are very familiar with the codebase.
