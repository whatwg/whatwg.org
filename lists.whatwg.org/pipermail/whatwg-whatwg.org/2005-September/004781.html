As the disabled content attribute[1] applies to more than just form controls,
namely the fieldset, option and optgroup elements the definition of both
:enabled and :disabled[2] should be changed to match that.

At the moment this definition mainly talks about form controls and fieldset
elements. Neither the option element or optgroup element is mentioned.


[1]<http://whatwg.org/specs/web-forms/current-work/#disabled>
[2]<http://whatwg.org/specs/web-forms/current-work/#relation>
