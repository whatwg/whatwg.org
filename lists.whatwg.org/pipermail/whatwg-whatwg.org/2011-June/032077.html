> quirks mode.
> 
> There is a bug on file at

Indeed. Right now I'm not changing the spec just because most browsers 
haven't changed the spec... I strongly encourage people who want this 
changed to advocate for it directly to the UA vendors who would need to 
update their code. If the browsers change the spec will obviously follow.
