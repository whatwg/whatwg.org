I was wrong, I didn't see what you mean. Turns out it's relatively easy to 
do today in canvas; after you've drawn your shape and filled it, just add 
the following code:

  c.save();
  c.clip();
  c.moveTo(0,0);
  c.lineTo(0,height);
  c.lineTo(width,height);
  c.lineTo(width,0);
  c.closePath();
  c.shadowColor = 'black';
  c.shadowBlur = 30;
  c.fill();
  c.restore();
