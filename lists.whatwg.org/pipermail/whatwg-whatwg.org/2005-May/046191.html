Regarding the Editing section, at the moment only contentEditable is
mentioned. While this is probably the most common method, designMode
should also be included.

Perhaps have two subsections, one for full document editing (designMode)
and the other for element content editing (contentEditable). There are two
well known implementations of designMode, in IE & Mozilla, and I think
that the Editing section should cover both off them, in effect get what is
already out there written down and specified in an non-proprietary spec.

Some useful designMode links:
http://msdn.microsoft.com/workshop/author/dhtml/reference/properties/desig
nmode.asp
http://www.mozilla.org/editor/midas-spec.html
