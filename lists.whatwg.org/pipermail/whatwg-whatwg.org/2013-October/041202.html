A few weeks ago there was a discussion on the

https://groups.google.com/a/chromium.org/forum/#!topic/graphics-dev/CQJXpXxO6dk

gain much traction. This time, I believe we have come up with compelling
requirements and use cases. Here is a wiki page with the proposal:
http://wiki.whatwg.org/wiki/Canvas_Context_Loss_and_Restoration

Please share your thoughts.
