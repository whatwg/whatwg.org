type", the UTF-16LE BOM is incorrectly listed as "FF FF" instead of "FF

correctly earlier in the text):

FF FF 00 00 	FF FF 00 00 	text/plain 	n/a 	UTF-16LE BOM
                   ^^
FF FF FF 00 	EF BB BF 00 	text/plain 	n/a 	UTF-8 BOM
