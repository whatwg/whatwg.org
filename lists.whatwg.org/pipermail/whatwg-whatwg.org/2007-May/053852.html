IE 5.2 (Mac)
Netscape 4, 8 (Windows)
Netscape 6, 7 (Mac)
Mozilla 1.7, Firefox 1.5, Firefox 2 (Mac)
Opera 3, 4, 5 (Windows)
Opera 6, 7, 8, 9 (Mac)
Safari 2.0.4 (Mac)
