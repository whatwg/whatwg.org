What are the exact terms of the act? Does it specifically mention 
scripting? It seems more plausible (to me, without reading the act) 
that it asserts that websites must be accessible to disabled people.

(principally those which plug in to some other browser such as IE). So 
it's not requiring js in itself that's problematic, it's uses of 
scripting that don't work well in non-visual browsers.
