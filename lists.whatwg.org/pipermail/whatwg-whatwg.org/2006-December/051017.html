The theme for XTech 2007 (15-18 May 2007 in Paris) is "The
Ubiquitous Web", and proposals are due by December 15 (this
Friday).

  http://xtech.expectnation.com/event/1/public/cfp/1

A list of the tracks and suggested topics is here:

  http://xtech.expectnation.com/event/1/public/content/tracks

Among the items in that list:

  - Emerging standards: XBL, CSS3, web applications standards
  - The future of HTML standards: XHTML, XForms, XUL,
    Microformats, WHATWG

For the proposal, all you need do is submit a short description
(25 words or so) along with an abstract (250 words or so). If you
want examples, see the schedule from this year's XTech (which
includes the short descriptions and abstracts):

  http://2006.xtech.org/schedule
