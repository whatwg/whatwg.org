Basically, I'm hoping to find out. I personally had a number of useful  
technical discussions on Google+. Maybe some of those will take place on  
the WHATWG Google+ page. Maybe some people prefer to get WHATWG pointers  
via Google+, just like some prefer to get it via Twitter today.

We started with email, branched out to IRC, got forums and wiki, a blog, a  
Twitter account. Each of those has proven successful I think. Not always  
to the same people, but I think that is the value. By giving people choice  
in how to participate we make it more easy for them to do so.
