I agree that the word "discourse" is more formal than "dialog", but it does cover both formal and informal speech unlike some other thesaurus inspired possibilities such as "colloquy" or "chat".  It does have the advantage over dialog of avoiding the spelling issue and the computer term issue.  If those issues end up being judged sufficient to make <dialog/> undesirable, then <discourse/> seems to me to be the best alternative available among English words.  Of course, if words were abandoned altogether <d/> would also be a possibility.
