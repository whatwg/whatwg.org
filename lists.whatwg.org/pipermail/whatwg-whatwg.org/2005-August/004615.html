Hi,

The first example on the a element[1] in WA1 should end with '</nav>' 
instead of '</navigation>'.

The first and the second example on application/x-www-form+xml: XML 
submission[2] are not well-formed XML, they should include '<input 
type="submit" value="Send"/>' instead of '<input type="submit" 
value="Send">'.

[1] http://whatwg.org/specs/web-apps/current-work/#the-a
[2] http://whatwg.org/specs/web-forms/current-work/#x-www-form-xml
