Hi,

The methods addText() and addPathByStrokingText() [1] take the NoInterfaceObject CanvasDrawingStyle [2] as argument. I wonder if that is even possible for WebIDL since there is no way to create it and no binding to ECMAScript is exposed. Shouldn't it rather be a DrawingStyle object[3], which indeed is an interface? I didn't scan if that is the case in other places as well. The definition of NoInterfaceObject is here [4].
