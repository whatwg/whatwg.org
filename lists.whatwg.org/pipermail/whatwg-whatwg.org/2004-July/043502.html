other: "-//WHATWG//DTD HTML 4.01 plus Web Forms 2.0//EN" 

.. matching the use of 'plus' in the XHTML1.1+SVG and XHTML 1.1+MathML 
2.0+SVG 1.1 DTD's, the use of 'HTML 4.01' in the HTML4 DTD, and the 
convention of separating the version number from the 'object' using a space. 

"-//W3C//DTD HTML 4.01//EN"

"-//W3C//DTD XHTML 1.1 plus MathML 2.0 plus SVG 1.1//EN"
