On Sun, 11 Jul 2004, Joshua Wise wrote:
>
> I was unclear as to whether WHAT-WG was only for HTML solutions at
> present, or for hypertext solutions as a whole. Can someone clear this
> up for me?

WHATWG members currently considers that only solutions based on HTML are
at all likely to be successful. This is largely based on the fact that
there are plenty of non-HTML solutions that are not successful, and a lot
of HTML- based solutions that _are_ successful.

So yeah, we're basing things on HTML.
