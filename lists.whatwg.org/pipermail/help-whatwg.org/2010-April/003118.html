According to RFC 3966 <http://www.rfc-editor.org/rfc/rfc3966.txt> you must write the telephone data as following.

<a href="tel:+31123456789">012 3456789 | +31 12 3456789</a>

See also: <http://www.softarmor.com/wgdb/docs/draft-brandner-tel-svc-00.txt> and <http://www.rfc-archive.org/getrfc.php?rfc=4759>.
