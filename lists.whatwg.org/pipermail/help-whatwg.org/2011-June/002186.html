Hi,

I would like to remind you for the HTML 5 Developer Group India Meetup on
2nd July 2011 (Saturday).

HTML 5 Developer Group India a group focused on clearing the mystery and
hype surrounding HTML 5 is organizing a *meetup on HTML 5 on 2nd July* at
OpenClass Meetup Point, 406, Surya Kiran Building, Kasturba Gandhi Marg,
Connaught Place, *New Delhi*.

*Shwetank Dixit* the *Web Evangelist at Opera* will be the main speaker.
Shwetank is very knowledgeable on HTML 5 and is also the member of W3C
Mobile Web for Social Development (MW4D) Group. For people who have been
thinking of diving into HTML 5 for some time this meetup is a no miss
opportunity.

I will also be speaking on OpenClass's experiences in porting old J2ME based
mobile game Fun2Hit to HTML 5. The game is live on Facebook at
http://apps.facebook.com/fun-two-hit/ .

If any one is interested to speak at the event or showcase their HTML 5
based web app or game then please let me know.
