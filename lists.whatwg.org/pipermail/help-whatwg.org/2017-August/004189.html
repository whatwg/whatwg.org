To unsubscribe:

# to help-unsubscribe at whatwg.org from  the address
# you want to unsubscribe, and then reply to the
#  confirmation e-mail you receive.

Based on the responses to my  suggestion to close this list and the
lack of activity over the years we've  decided to go ahead and do it.
We'll keep the main whatwg at whatwg.org  mailing list, described  here:
https://whatwg.org/mailing-list#specs.

You should all be aware  that most discussion about standards takes
place on GitHub these days, over  at the repositories linked from
https://github.com/whatwg. While it's a bit  of a loss to use
centralized software, overall we believe it has helped  tremendously in
getting more folks involved in standards  development.

You're also welcome to join us on IRC, at #whatwg on  Freenode.

Thanks for your participation and hope to run into you all  again on
whatwg@, IRC, or  GitHub!
