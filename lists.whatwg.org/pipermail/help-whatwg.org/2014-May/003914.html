>
> Thoughts? Should I consider crafting my documents more like I did in
> HTML4 (for the sake of SEO and screen readers)???
Using the new sectioning elements (including the use of h1 exclusively), 
does not effect SEO in my experience. Variables such as Page Authority 
and pagerank carry much more weight than markup – its a surprise to see 
what some top sites get away with!

It can be good to watch a few of Matt Cutt's videos on YouTube, he works 
at Google, and has cleared up a lot of markup questions.

I think a lot of misconceptions can be avoided simply by referring to 
the standard as HTML, rather than HTML5..

I can't say i personally care about screen readers, and besides, many of 
these accessibility evangelists tend to be a bit extreme in their views.
