I have a quick question about browser video support. There's a lot of

so far:

3gpp: video/3gpp
3g2: video/3gpp2

f4a: audio/mp4
f4b: audio/mp4
f4p: video/mp4
f4v: video/mp4
mov: video/quicktime
m4a: audio/mp4
m4b: audio/mp4
m4p: audio/mp4
m4v: video/mp4
mkv: video/x-matroska
mp4: video/mp4
mp3: audio/mp3
ogg: audio/ogg
ogv: video/ogg
webm: video/webm
