I need to override the default behaviour of the video tag's poster attribute, which is:
[http://www.w3.org/TR/html5/video.html#attr-video-poster] 
>> Notwithstanding the above, the poster frame should be preferred over nothing, but the poster frame should not be shown again after a frame of video has been shown. <<

My intended behaviour is to show the poster image after the video played as well.
The reasoning behind it is that there are a number of videos on a JS driven carousel and vanishing poster frames render the navigation on this carousel useless if the user  played even a few seconds of a particular video.
